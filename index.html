fun fact 1

Zlatan has played for many top clubs throughout his career, including Ajax, Juventus, Inter Milan, Barcelona, Paris Saint-Germain, Manchester United, and AC Milan.

fun fact 2

He is known for his incredible athleticism and acrobatic goals, which have earned him the nickname "Ibracadabra".

fun fact 3

Zlatan has a black belt in taekwondo and has even incorporated some martial arts moves into his celebrations after scoring goals.

fun act 4

In 2011, Zlatan released his autobiography "I am Zlatan Ibrahimović" which quickly became a bestseller in Sweden.
